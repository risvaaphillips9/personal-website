These are the projects I have completed during the Summer 2025 Jumpstart program prior to starting my Masters Degree in Information Systems at Indiana University Bloomington. Corporate Programs Management System

Our Project

This project required us to demonstrate a complete understanding of the information systems development lifecycle (SDLC). Our prototype focused on building a system that manages corporate training programs offered by the university. The solution enables administrators to create courses, upload student data, auto-assign students to course sections, and generate dynamic enrollment reports, all while ensuring scalability and clean data architecture.

Big Points

  • Python Console Application: Core logic for adding courses, enrolling students, and generating reports.
  • HTML Interface: Fully navigational frontend allowing users to upload data files and view summaries.
  • PostgreSQL Database Schema: Robust relational design supporting multiple programs, cohorts, and sections with referential integrity.
  • Presentation Deck & Documentation: Included use case diagrams, ERD, sequence diagrams, and user stories.

Global Market Entry Strategy

Our Project

The project focused on developing a comprehensive market entry strategy for a new product entering the U.S. market. Working as part of a four-person consulting team, we analyzed emerging consumer trends, benchmarked successful case studies, and identified feasible positioning strategies for a premium international product seeking entry into competitive retail and hospitality channels.

Big Points

  • Full market and competitor analysis
  • Consumer segmentation profiles and persona grids
  • Strategic recommendations for brand positioning, partnerships, and experiential marketing
  • Final client presentation summarizing insights, analysis, and implementation roadmap
