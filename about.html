My Journey at IU

Coming from Dubai to IU has been an extremely insightful journey for me because I was able to work, collaborate, and communicate with students of diverse cultures, ideas, and values.

I have been able to find my passion in technology because of the support, guidance, and resources I received here at IU. The courses I have taken are very unique and I have been able to learn and refine my technical skills through them.
